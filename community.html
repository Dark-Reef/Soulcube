Community

Community is a strong part of what makes Soulcube, Soulcube! We're all about getting more people into game development and giving those people ways to connect with others who share intrests!

We run a weekly podcast under the name of "Soulcast" where we engage with the community, discussing many various topics & answering your questions! These topics are generally drawn from another weekly event we run across all our social platforms - Tell us Tuesday. Every week we put out a topic up for discussion & the participant with the most interesting answer scores themselves a shoutout across all socials!

>

Every month, from the First Saturday to the Last, the Super Sarturday Art/Music comp runs & is open to anyone across the globe! Simply choose a word from each row of the monthly grid & construct either a visual artwork or a song using the contructued 2 word prompt! The winner from both the Art & Music divisions gets to choose between a prize of a $10 steam card or a month of discord nitro.


Filling out the weeks inbetween Super Sarturday, weekly Sarturday events run with the type of prompts cycling through each week. Sometimes its linework restrictions, silhouette restrictions or even pallete restrictions!
