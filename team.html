Team

Co-founder of Soulcube, programmer, musician, writer & passionate game designer, Jade has been developing games alongside Maxwell since moving back down to the Australian South East Coast.

He has developed a passion for helping others get into game dev through both local & online means. He has a strong passion for literature & is a big believer in the validity of games as art. Jade also works as a casual software developer for 2pi Software, Soulcubes partner in education.

Favourite games include Risk of Rain 2, Firewatch, Gris & What Remains of Edith finch.
